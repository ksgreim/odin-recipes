Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti carbonara is a delicious pasta dish. It features a creamy sauce made from eggs, hard cheese, cured pork, and black pepper. It is sure to wow family and friends alike.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Cured Pork
  • Eggs
  • Cheese - Pecorino Romano or Parmesan
  • Black Pepper
  • Pasta Water

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, fry the cured pork until crisp.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs, grated cheese, and black pepper
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving some starchy water.
  5. Combine the hot pasta with the pork and its rendered fat in a pan (off the heat)
  6. Quickly add the egg/cheese mixture, tossing vigorously. The residual heat from the pasta cooks the eggs into a creamy sauce.
  7. Add splashes of pasta water as needed to achieve a smooth, glossy consistency.

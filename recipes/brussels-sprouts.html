Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Description

This recipe will make you love brussels sprouts. It is simple and delicious. Top them off with some bacon bits and/or parmesan cheese to level it up.

Ingredients

  • Brussels sprouts rinsed
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and any other seasonings of your choice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F and line baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cut ends off sprouts and cut in half. Remove excess leaves.
  3. Toss sprouts with oil, vinegar, and seasonings.
  4. Place sprouts cut-side down on baking sheet. Roast for 18-20 minutes, or until bottoms of sprouts are golden brown
  5. Enjoy!
